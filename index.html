What this site is

This is a map of my mahi. I operate across several areas — research, clinical health services, natural health products, and community governance — all connected by a single purpose: improving health outcomes for Māori.

Each area has its own organisations and projects. This site explains how they connect and points you in the right direction depending on your interest.
