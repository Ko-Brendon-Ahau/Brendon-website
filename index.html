Guided by a commitment to Hauora Māori, I am working across research, clinical practice, natural health, and community leadership to contribute to equitable outcomes for our whānau.

Explore my mahi

One Kaupapa, Many Spaces

No single approach will improve Māori health outcomes; it takes mahi across many spaces.

This is where the different strands of my lifes mahi, my purpose, come together.

From research and clinical care, to natural health products, community kaupapa like our kōhaka reo, and wider system-level clinical and community governance; each space plays a role. None stand alone, but together they reflect a shared focus on supporting better outcomes for our people.

If you’re curious about any part of my journey, this site will help you explore it, and see how the pieces connect.
