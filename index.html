He aha te kaupapa?

One kaupapa, many pathways

The mahi here spans pharmacy, research, natural health, education, governance, and community. On the surface they look different. But they all connect back to the same question: how do we improve health outcomes for whānau Māori?

Each pathway is a different lever — clinical, systemic, educational, relational. Together they form an ecosystem, not a list of jobs.
