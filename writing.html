Writing

Reflections, research translation, and thinking out loud about hauora.

I write to make sense of things — and to share that sense-making with others. The topics tend to circle around Māori health, systems thinking, medicines, natural health, and what it means to pursue pae ora in practice.

Some pieces are research translation. Some are reflections. Some are just things I needed to work through in writing.
