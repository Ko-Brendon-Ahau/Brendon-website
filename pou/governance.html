Shaping the conditions

Hauora is shaped long before someone walks into a clinic.

Why governance matters

Health outcomes are shaped by systems — by how services are funded, designed, and delivered. By who gets a seat at the table when decisions are made. By whether the people most affected by a system have any power to change it.

Governance work is about being in those rooms. Contributing to decisions that affect how health services are structured, how resources are allocated, and whether Māori communities have genuine influence over the systems that affect them.
