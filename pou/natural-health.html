Pou 03 — Natural Health

Supporting the body differently

Products grounded in lived experience and emerging science — built for how people actually live.

The thinking behind it

Alongside medicines, there's a growing body of evidence — and a long tradition of Indigenous knowledge — pointing to the role of natural compounds in supporting health. Stress, sleep, inflammation, recovery — these are areas where natural health products can make a real difference, when they're well-made and well-explained.

My interest in this space comes from both clinical experience and personal conviction. I've seen what happens when people have access to good information and good products. And I've seen what happens when they don't.

The approach

Both projects are built on the same principles:

  • Grounded in evidence — not hype
  • Accessible — in price, language, and availability
  • Honest about what works and what doesn't
  • Respectful of both science and Indigenous knowledge
