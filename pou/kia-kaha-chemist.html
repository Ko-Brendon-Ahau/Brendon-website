Pou 02 — Clinical Health

Kia Kaha Chemist

Meeting whānau where they are — in their homes, their communities, and their realities.

What it is

Kia Kaha Chemist is a mobile and online pharmacy service built around the idea that access, trust, and understanding matter just as much as the medicine itself.

Rather than waiting for people to come to a pharmacy, we go to them — into homes, onto marae, and into community spaces. We have real conversations about medicines, health, and what's actually going on in people's lives.

Why it exists

Early in my career, I started to see a pattern. Whānau who weren't taking their medicines — not because they didn't care, but because no one had taken the time to explain them properly. Kaumātua managing multiple prescriptions without understanding what each one was for. People who felt judged or rushed in clinical settings, and so stopped going.

Kia Kaha Chemist is a response to that. It's built on the belief that when people feel seen, heard, and respected — and when health information is delivered in a way that makes sense to them — they're better equipped to make decisions about their own hauora.

What we do

  • Mobile pharmacy visits — homes, marae, community spaces
  • Online pharmacy services
  • Medicine reviews and health conversations
  • Support for whānau managing long-term conditions
